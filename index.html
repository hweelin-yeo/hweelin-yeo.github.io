About me

Hi! I'm currently pursuing my Master of Enginering in Computer Science in Cornell Tech, NYC. Areas of interest in Computer Science includes Operating Systems, Cloud Computing and Distributed Systems. I am also experienced in mobile (iOS) and software engineering, and enjoy the intersection between product and technology. I love creating products from users and hearing from them.

Graduated with a B.A in Computer Science from Cornell University, so ask me anything about Upstate New York and crazy winters! Originally from Singapore. Love dogs and hope to have 3 in the future.
